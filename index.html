Reuse, Repurpose and Recycle

Away from the landfill and into the hands of those in need. We encourange people to recycle and donate the education essentails and supplies that they no longer need to those less fortunate.

Developing a resource centre

We are currently engaging in discussion with government, city missions, pastoral care teams, council and teachers/support teachers to obtain and identify essential needs for studentss across the nation.
